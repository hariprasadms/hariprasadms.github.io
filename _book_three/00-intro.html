People think that love stories are about finding the right person. Amma and Nanna taught me that sometimes it is about finding the right person at the wrong time…and then being able to find them again when life gives you a second chance. ❤️

This one is for the people I love the most. My Amma and Nanna.

A Note from Author

First of all, I would like to thank my amazing Nanna - the hero of our story, for always inspiring me and loving me more than anyone else can ever do. You are my inspiration and I will always look up to you.

Secondly, I would like to thank my wonderful mom for always being there for me. You are my strength, the candle in my darkness, my best friend, and one of my biggest mentors.

You both taught me how to stay happy in all situations. Whatever I am is because of both of you. This small venture in the book writing world is like a small gift to both of you for your endless love.

Also, thanks to my dad, for giving this wonderful opportunity for Amma and Nanna to meet. 😉

I love you both immensely. 💕

Your loving elder daughter
