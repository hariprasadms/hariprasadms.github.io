It has been a few days after Krish’s birthday. We both have become friends. Kind of. He says hello when he sees me in the morning. We drink coffee together etc.

Right now, I am waiting for Kiran. My fiancé. On the stairs of my office. It is getting late, and he said he will pick me up half an hour ago.

I love him but, I never understand him. Sometimes, he acts like the best fiancé in the entire world. But sometimes, he is a nightmare. Sometimes, I feel like breaking this engagement. Like yesterday. He went to Goa without telling me with his friends.

For four days.

I was so worried.

But what can I do. I have said yes to him. I am engaged. Maybe he will change. He will love me. He will listen. Maybe he will stay.

I am so busy in my thought that I didn’t notice the footsteps behind me.

Krish.

He sits beside me and says-

“Hey! What are you doing here?”

“Just waiting for someone.” I say and smile.

“Where are you going?” he enquires, returning me a smile.

“Just till the circle.”

“Oh! I am going there too. I can drop you. It is getting dark. It is not safe for you to stay here.” He speaks.

He is right it is getting dark and it is definitely not safe for me to stay here. I don’t even think Srini remembers to pick me up.

So, I say okay. He drops me till the circle and asks me if he should drop me home. I refuse and tell him that I will take the bus. After 10 minutes of arguing he agrees. Only on one condition. He gave me his number and asked me to give a missed call after I reach home.

****

It’s been ten minutes that I came home. I pick up my phone and call him. “Hello, who is this?” he asks, and from his voice I can say that he knows it is me.

I say- “Hi. This is Radhe. You told me to call once I reach home.”

We end up talking for hours. He asks me all about what I like and what I don’t like. I tell him about my love for chocolates and how I cannot resist one.

The next day I sit on my desk, checking my emails.

He comes from behind, pats my head and leaves three chocolates on my table.

And leaves.

Without a word.

Just leaves.

Then, out of nowhere, I get a message from him in Teams.

Three words.

You are cute.

What?
