Kriti

Kriti is a tenth-grade student from India and a budding author with a deep love for stories. When It Comes Back to Us is her debut — a love story inspired by the people closest to her heart. When she isn’t writing, she’s lost in books, music, and the small everyday moments that become the heart of her stories.
