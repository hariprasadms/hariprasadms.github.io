The next few days automatically become the best days of my life. Watching Radhe laugh. The way she frowned when she was concentrating. It was nice. But I never seem to have a chance to speak to her.

Ashwini notices me while I am in my inner monologue.

“You like that girl. Don’t you?” she says

“What?”

“Yeah. Radhe. You like her. The way you look at her, it is quite easy to understand.”

“If it is that easy, why don’t you help me to talk to her?” I say jokingly.

Like she was waiting for me to say this, Ashwini jumps out of her chair and goes behind Radhe, who was going for a coffee and says-

“Hey Radhe! Why don’t you come sit with us?”

After that we talk for hours about where she is from, her education and everything she likes.

She is Telugu. She is also very educated.

The conversation that I had with her only seemed to make me like her more.

Who would have imagined that I would like a girl this much, only after two weeks of meeting her.

Today is my birthday. My 28th birthday. Ashwini had a plan. She called all our colleagues including Radhe to an ice cream shop as a birthday treat.

We all spoke and ate a lot of ice cream, and the whole time I could not take my eyes of Radhe

After the “party”, we had to go back to office.

As I sat on my bike, Ranveer started climbing behind me. I was really hoping for Radhe to come with me.

But luckily, Ashwini came to the rescue, and I will thank her for the rest of my life. She said-

“Ranveer, why don’t you go on that Activa and let Radhe go with Krish?”

“Not fair! It is always me who is ignored. I hate you guys!!” he grumbles, as he takes the keys.

I glance at Radhe who shyly sits behind me.

This is the best birthday of my life.
