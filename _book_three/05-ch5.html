I had convinced myself I was being careful.

Careful with my feelings.

Careful with my heart.

Careful around Radhe.

But love has a way of growing quietly.

It starts with coffee breaks.

Lunch conversations.

Shared jokes.

And before you realize it, one person becomes your favourite part of every day.

That person was Radhe.

And I was tired of pretending she wasn't.

I am sitting in our favourite bakery.

She is sitting front of me, and I am staring at her. Finally, I gather up the courage and say- “I have been wanting to tell you something from a really long time.”

“What is it?” she asks confused.

“I love you. I really love you. Every time something good happens, you're the first person I want to tell. When I walk into the office, I look for you. When you're absent, the whole place feels different. Will you marry me?"

She looks shocked. Then she says in a very low voice- “I didn’t know you…. I am so sorry. I am committed Krish. I am engaged.” I stared at her.

Trying to process what she'd said.

Trying to understand how I had missed something so important.

I looked away.

Unable to breathe.

Unable to think.

I don’t know what to say. Out of all the responses I imagined, this was the most unexpected. She is engaged. I don’t want to lose her. I simply can’t lose her. I say-Finally I looked at her.

"Do you love him?"

The question escaped before I could stop it

Radhe froze.

For one brief second.

A second that lasted forever.

Then she nodded.

A tiny nod.

Almost invisible.

But enough.

My heart shattered.

Because even if there was hesitation in her eyes...

Even if there was sadness in her voice...

She had already chosen her future.

And it wasn't me.

Even after all of this, I can’t imagine a life without her, so I say-

“I didn’t know that. I thought… Radhe I don’t want to lose you. Let us be friends like before.”

She nods.

That’s it. A single sentence changed everything.
