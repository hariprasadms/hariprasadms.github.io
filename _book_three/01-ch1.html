Today seems like a perfectly predictable day. Wake up. Go to office. Drink coffee. Finish deadlines. Go home. Repeat. Nothing exciting. Nothing dramatic. Just the same old routine I have been following since the past two years. I wake up and drag myself out of bed.

As I reach to my office, I see that it is unusually busy. Ashwini (my colleague) told us that few new employees will be joining us. Nobody cared. New people came and went all the time.

I was sitting at my desk, pretending to work while secretly checking cricket scores, when I heard laughter near the entrance.

A soft laugh.

Bright.

Unfiltered.

The kind that makes people look up automatically.

Including me.

I glanced toward the reception area.

And froze.

A girl stood there talking to the manager. She was wearing a light pink top. No dramatic makeup. Her light brown eyes held a calm warmth, like sunlight filtered through quiet mornings. Her face was delicately oval, balanced and gentle in its lines. Her long hair cascading down her shoulders. Then, I noticed that she gets dimples when she laughs, and at that moment, I knew I was done for.

I try to concentrate on my work again, but Anita (manager)Comes in front of our table along with the cute girl and says- “Hello everyone. This is Radhe. She will be joining you guys from today.” Radhe. I think. Such a nice name. Anitha goes on to introduce the other two guys, Ranveer and Chetan. But that goes in a blur, because all my attention is on Radhe.
