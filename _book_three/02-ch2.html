Today is my first day in my new office. I am a bit nervous, but also very excited. I have always dreamed of this day. My first day of working. I am standing in the reception area with my college friends Ranveer and Chetan who got the same campus placement as me.

Suddenly, Ranveer says from behind me - “"Interesting."

I didn't even look up.

"What?"

Ranveer smirked.

"Nothing."

"Ranveer."

He leaned closer.

"The guy on that desk"

I glanced up.

"Which guy?"

"The one who's been staring at you for the last ten minutes."

"What?"

Ranveer laughed.

"Don't act innocent."

I immediately looked across the room.

And found him.

Krish.

After Anita introduced us, we had shaken hands.

He was sitting at his desk.

But the moment our eyes met, he quickly looked away.

Almost guiltily.

My stomach did a strange little flip.

when I looked back toward my screen, I couldn't stop thinking about it.

Was he?

No.

Surely not.

He seemed quiet.

Reserved type.

Like the type of guy who would listen.

I found myself glancing in his direction again.

Just once.

Purely for scientific purposes.

And immediately regretted it.

Because now I noticed things.

Like how ridiculously tall he was.

Or how his smile appeared unexpectedly and completely transformed his face.

He was handsome.

Then, I remind myself that it was probably nothing. I am already engaged. Mybe I am overthinking it. He probably was not even looking at me.

Right?
