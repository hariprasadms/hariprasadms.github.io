Radhe got married last month. I am still not able to process it.

The girl who I loved so much, is now someone else’s wife. I should stop thinking about us.

But the problem is that, the whole world teaches you how to fall in love, but no one ever tells you how to come out of it and forget.

My family is also asking me to get married.

So, that’s what I am going to do. I am going to move on and get married. Settle down and forget about her.

It is not that big of a deal. I am not a college student to think about this so much. But then why am I feeling so shattered?

***

My parents have found a match. Her name is Soumya. She is beautiful. She is independent and good. We are going to get married in a month. Now I can finally forget Radhe.

Right?
