Welcome

Every app you have ever loved — and every app that has ever let you down — was shaped by an invisible craft called software testing. This is a story about that craft, told through the life of one engineer who began by getting it completely wrong.

From Bugs to Brilliance follows Arun across the most important years of his career: from a confident young developer who breaks a bank's login for forty-one thousand people, to a quality leader who learns to work side by side with thinking machines.

Each chapter hides a real testing idea inside a very human story — no jargon, no lectures. Just one person slowly learning to see software the way the best testers do: not as screens, but as promises made to strangers.

You can read it like a book, or press Listen on any chapter to hear it read aloud.

The people you'll meet

A handful of people shape Arun's journey. You'll meet them again and again.

Arun — the hero of our story. A bright, slightly over-confident new graduate who learns, the hard way, that making software work and making it safe are two very different things.

Meera — a calm, sharp senior tester, and the mentor Arun never expected. She rarely hands him answers. Instead she asks the one question nobody else in the room thought to ask.

Priya — a senior manager who believes quality is everyone's job, and isn't afraid to say so in a room full of proud engineers.

Rohan — a talented, doubting developer who thinks testing is beneath him. His scorn keeps pushing Arun to think harder than he wants to.

Their story runs from the very first ideas of testing all the way to the age of artificial intelligence. Turn the page, and start where Arun started — on his first day.
